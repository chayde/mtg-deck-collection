# Simic Omniscience (Standard Kona Combo)
**Archetype:** Simic Combo / Big Mana
**Format:** Standard (January 2026)
**Power Level:** Tier 1

## The Combo
1.  **Survive:** Use *Unauthorized Exit* and *Dispelling Exhale* to stay alive.
2.  **Dig:** Use *Consult the Star Charts* and *Stock Up* to find **Kona, Rescue Beastie** and **Omniscience**.
3.  **The Trigger:** Cast Kona. On the next turn, attack or use a "Tapping" effect.
4.  **The Drop:** Put *Omniscience* into play for free.
5.  **The Win:** Cast your entire hand for free, including *Uthros, Titanic Godcore* and *Roiling Dragonstorm*.
