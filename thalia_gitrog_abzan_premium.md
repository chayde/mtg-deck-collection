# Thalia and The Gitrog Monster (Abzan Landfall & Hatebears) - Premium Version
**Commander:** Thalia and The Gitrog Monster
**Colors:** Abzan (White / Green / Black)
**Power Level:** Bracket 3.5 (High Power / Optimized)
**Budget:** ~$850 USD
**Focus:** Landfall, Creature Sacrifice, Combat Control

This is the uncompromised version of the deck. It utilizes the best-in-slot cards for maximum consistency, power, and oppression. It includes premium staples like *Sheoldred, the Apocalypse*, *The Great Henge*, and *Vampiric Tutor* to ensure you always have the answer you need.
