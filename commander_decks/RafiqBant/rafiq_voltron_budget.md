# Rafiq of the Many: Exalted Voltron

## 1. Project Overview
**Commander:** [[Rafiq of the Many]]
**Colors:** Bant (Green, White, Blue)
**Strategy:** Voltron / Exalted. Aim to win through Commander damage by attacking with a single, highly-buffed creature (usually Rafiq himself) that gains Double Strike.

## 3. Strategy Guide
* **Early Game:** Ramp aggressively. Look for *Puresteel Paladin* or *Sram* to set up a draw engine.
* **Mid Game:** Deploy "Paladins" like *Mirran Crusader* or *Silverblade Paladin*. Use *Bower Passage* or *Whiptongue Hydra* to clear the skies.
* **Finishing:** Rafiq + *Finest Hour* or *Kinsbaile Cavalier* ensures massive damage. *Steel of the Godhead* is the primary finisher aura.
