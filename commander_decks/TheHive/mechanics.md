# Magic: The Gathering Mechanics & Keywords Glossary

This file serves as a reference for keywords and abilities encountered in the "The Hive" Sliver deck.

## A
*   **Afflict N:** Whenever this creature becomes blocked, defending player loses N life. (Source: *Lazotep Sliver*)
*   **Amass [Subtype] N:** Put N +1/+1 counters on an Army you control. It's also a [Subtype]. If you don't control an Army, create a 0/0 black [Subtype] Army creature token first. (Source: *Lazotep Sliver*)

## C
*   **Cascade:** When you cast this spell, exile cards from the top of your library until you exile a nonland card that costs less. You may cast it without paying its mana cost. Put the exiled cards on the bottom of your library in a random order. (Source: *The First Sliver*)
*   **Changeling:** This card is every creature type. (Source: *Amoeboid Changeling*)

## D
*   **Double Strike:** This creature deals both first-strike and regular combat damage. (Source: *Bonescythe Sliver*)

## F
*   **Flash:** You may cast this spell any time you could cast an instant. (Source: *Quick Sliver*)
*   **Flying:** This creature can't be blocked except by creatures with flying or reach. (Source: *Galerider Sliver*, *Winged Sliver*)

## H
*   **Haste:** This creature can attack and {T} as soon as it comes under your control. (Source: *Blur Sliver*, *Heart Sliver*)

## I
*   **Indestructible:** Damage and effects that say "destroy" don't destroy this creature. If its toughness is 0 or less, it's still put into its owner's graveyard. (Source: *Sliver Hivelord*)

## L
*   **Lifelink:** Damage dealt by this creature also causes you to gain that much life. (Source: *Syphon Sliver*)

## M
*   **Menace:** This creature can't be blocked except by two or more creatures. (Source: *Blightstep Pathway* - N/A, usually found on specific Slivers not currently in main list, but good to know).

## P
*   **Poisonous N:** Whenever this creature deals combat damage to a player, that player gets N poison counters. (Source: *Virulent Sliver*)
*   **Protection from [Quality]:** This creature can't be blocked, targeted, dealt damage, enchanted, or equipped by anything that has [Quality]. (Source: *Ward Sliver*)

## R
*   **Reach:** This creature can block creatures with flying. (Source: *Cloudshredder Sliver*)
*   **Regenerate:** The next time this creature would be destroyed this turn, it isn't. Instead tap it, remove all damage from it, and remove it from combat. (Source: *Crypt Sliver*)
*   **Replicate [Cost]:** When you cast this spell, copy it for each time you paid its replicate cost. (Source: *Hatchery Sliver*)
*   **Riot:** This creature enters the battlefield with your choice of a +1/+1 counter or haste. (Source: *Rhythm of the Wild*)

## S
*   **Shadow:** This creature can block or be blocked by only creatures with shadow. (Source: *Shifting Sliver* essentially grants unblockable, but actual Shadow keyword exists on *Shadow Sliver* - effectively similar).
*   **Shroud:** This permanent can't be the target of spells or abilities. (Source: *Crystalline Sliver*)
*   **Slivercycling [Cost]:** [Cost], Discard this card: Search your library for a Sliver card, reveal it, put it into your hand, then shuffle. (Source: *Homing Sliver*)

## V
*   **Vigilance:** Attacking doesn't cause this creature to tap. (Source: *Sentinel Sliver*)
