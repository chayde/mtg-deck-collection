## 1. The Commander: The First Sliver
*   **Mana Cost:** {W}{U}{B}{R}{G} (5 CMC)
*   **Ability:** **Cascade** (When you cast this, reveal cards from top until you hit a nonland costing <5. Cast it for free.)
*   **Effect:** All Sliver spells you cast have **Cascade**.
*   **Strategy:** This is a "Snowball" commander. Casting a 6-drop Sliver cascades into a 5-drop, which cascades into a 4-drop, etc. You don't need to attack to win; you just need to cast spells.

## 2. The Sliver Army (Creature Analysis)

### A. The "Lords" (Stat Buffs)
*   **Megantic Sliver** ({3}{G}{G}): Slivers get **+3/+3**. The biggest stat stick.
*   **Muscle / Sinew / Predatory Slivers** ({1}{G} / {1}{W}): Slivers get **+1/+1**. The efficient basics.
*   **Sliver Legion** (Wishlist/Fetchable): Slivers get +1/+1 for *each other* Sliver. Finisher.

### B. The "Evasion" Team (Keywords)
*   **Galerider / Winged Sliver** ({U}): Grants **Flying**. Essential for getting damage through.
*   **Cloudshredder Sliver** ({R}{W}): Grants **Flying** and **Haste**. One of the best cards in the deck.
*   **Shifting Sliver** ({3}{U}): Slivers **can't be blocked** (except by other Slivers). A "I win" button.
*   **Ward Sliver** ({4}{W}): Protection from a chosen color. Makes you unblockable/untargetable by that color.

### C. The "Protection" Team (Defensive)
*   **Crystalline Sliver** ({W}{U}): Grants **Shroud**. No one can target your Slivers (including you). High priority protection.
*   **Sliver Hivelord** ({W}{U}{B}{R}{G}): Grants **Indestructible**. Immune to "Destroy" board wipes.
*   **Hibernation Sliver** ({U}{B}): Pay 2 life to return a Sliver to hand. Saves them from exile/wipes.
*   **Crypt Sliver** ({1}{B}): Tap to **Regenerate**. Saves from combat death/destruction.
*   **Root Sliver** ({3}{G}): Slivers **can't be countered**. Essential vs Blue decks.

### D. The "Mana" Team (Ramp)
*   **Gemhide / Manaweft Sliver** ({1}{G}): All Slivers become mana dorks (Tap for any color). Explosive ramp.

### E. The "Utility" Team (Removal & Value)
*   **Harmonic Sliver** ({1}{G}{W}): ETB destroy artifact/enchantment. Be careful: it's mandatory!
*   **Necrotic Sliver** ({1}{W}{B}): {3}, Sac: Destroy **target permanent**. Turns every Sliver into a "Vindicate".
*   **Toxin / Venom Sliver**: **Deathtouch** (or similar). Kills anything they block/block them.
*   **Homing Sliver** ({2}{R}): **Slivercycling {3}**. Discard to tutor any Sliver.
*   **Lazotep Sliver** ({1}{B}{R}): **Afflict 2** / **Amass 2**. (Currently missing).

## 3. Non-Creature Support

### Ramp & Fixing
*   **Arcane Signet, Sol Ring, Chromatic Lantern**: Standard mana rocks. Lantern fixes all colors.
*   **Three Visits, Nature's Lore, Farseek**: Green ramp spells finding specific land types (Triomes/Shocks).
*   **Urza's Incubator / Herald's Horn**: Cost reducers. Incubator makes Slivers cost {2} less.

### Card Advantage
*   **Guardian Project / Beast Whisperer**: Draw a card whenever a creature enters/cast.
*   **Kindred Discovery**: (Wishlist/Maybeboard) - Massive draw engine.
*   **Descendants' Path**: Free casts from top of library if it matches creature type.
*   **Sliver Hive**: Land that taps for colored mana for Slivers, or creates 1/1 tokens.

### Protection & Recursion
*   **Patriarch's Bidding**: Return ALL Slivers from graveyard to play. The ultimate recovery spell after a board wipe.
*   **Teferi's Protection / Heroic Intervention**: Phase out or Indestructible at instant speed.

## 4. Key Combos & Interactions
1.  **Sliver Queen + Mana Echoes (Wishlist)**: Infinite Mana / Infinite Tokens.
2.  **Sliver Overlord + Amoeboid Changeling**:
    *   Tap Amoeboid to make opponent's creature a "Sliver".
    *   Pay {3} to Overlord: "Gain control of target Sliver".
    *   Result: Steal any creature permanently.
3.  **The First Sliver + 1-Drop Slivers**:
    *   Normally you cascade *into* a 1-drop.
    *   You cannot cascade *from* a 1-drop (since nothing costs 0 in the deck usually).
    *   However, `Ancestral Vision` or `Profane Tutor` (if added) could be hit by 1-drops.
