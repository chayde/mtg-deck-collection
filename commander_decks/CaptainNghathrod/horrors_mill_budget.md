# Captain N'ghathrod (Budget Horror Mill)
**Commander:** Captain N'ghathrod
**Colors:** Dimir (Blue / Black)
**Power Level:** Bracket 2 (Focused)
**Budget:** ~$90 USD (Budget Optimized)
**Focus:** Horror Tribal, Combat Mill, Theft
**Game Plan:** Attack with evasive Horrors to trigger the Captain's mill ability. At the end of your turn, steal the best Artifact or Creature you milled. Use your opponents' strength against them.

## Commander
*   1x [Captain N'ghathrod](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/646/captain-nghathrod)

## The Horror Squad (Combat & Mill Triggers)
These creatures are Horrors (or synergize with them) and have evasion or powerful effects.
*   1x [Consuming Aberration](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/840/consuming-aberration) (The MVP. Mills until a land whenever you cast a spell. Grows huge.)
*   1x [Sludge Monster](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/735/sludge-monster) (Turns threats into 2/2 slimes.)
*   1x [Hullbreaker Horror](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/724/hullbreaker-horror) (Bounces spells and permanents. A powerhouse control piece.)
*   1x [Toxrill, the Corrosive](https://scryfall.com/card/vow/132/toxrill-the-corrosive) (Board wipe on a stick. Wait, check price... $15-$20. Might be too high. Replacement: **Overseer of the Damned**.)
*   1x [Overseer of the Damned](https://scryfall.com/card/mic/127/overseer-of-the-damned) (Kills creature, makes zombies.)
*   1x [Nemesis of Reason](https://scryfall.com/card/2xm/209/nemesis-of-reason) (Mills 10 on attack.)
*   1x [Guiltfeeder](https://scryfall.com/card/mh2/273/guiltfeeder) (Loses life based on GY size. Unblockable-ish.)
*   1x [Brainstealer Dragon](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/670/brainstealer-dragon) (Flying horror. Steals cards from top of decks.)
*   1x [Aboleth Spawn](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/662/aboleth-spawn) (Copies ETB abilities of opponents' creatures. Including Angels!)
*   1x [Uchuulon](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/673/uchuulon) (Crab horror that makes copies of itself from opponent's GY.)
*   1x [Grell Philosopher](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/666/grell-philosopher) (Horrors gain activated abilities of artifacts.)
*   1x [Mind Flayer](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/728/mind-flayer) (Steals a creature permanently.)
*   1x [Nighthowler](https://scryfall.com/card/znc/49/nighthowler) (Huge stats based on GYs.)
*   1x [Sewer Nemesis](https://scryfall.com/card/cmr/152/sewer-nemesis) (Huge stats.)
*   1x [Zellix, Sanity Flayer](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/652/zellix-sanity-flayer) (Makes Horror tokens when players mill creatures.)
*   1x [Ravenous Chupacabra](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/770/ravenous-chupacabra) (Removal on a Horror body.)
*   1x [Phyrexian Revoker](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/868/phyrexian-revoker) (Stops activated abilities. Is a Horror!)
*   1x [Wharf Infiltrator](https://scryfall.com/card/emn/80/wharf-infiltrator) (Looter + Makes tokens.)
*   1x [Changeling Outcast](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/743/changeling-outcast) (Unblockable Horror.)
*   1x [Elder Brain](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/125/elder-brain) (Exiles hands and lets you play them!)
*   1x [Intellect Devourer](https://scryfall.com/card/clb/132/intellect-devourer) (Steals body from hand.)
*   1x [Arvinox, the Mind Flail](https://scryfall.com/card/slx/1/arvinox-the-mind-flail) (Cast opponents' spells.)
